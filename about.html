About Voltcontrol Engineering

Power Resistor & Neutral Grounding Solutions

Who We Are

Voltcontrol Engineering Pvt. Ltd., founded in 2023, brings over 12 years of expertise in the Power Resistor industry. We serve industrial and utility sectors by delivering proven, reliable and application-specific resistor solutions.

Our product range includes Neutral Grounding Resistors (NGR), high and low resistance grounding systems, neutral isolation switch panels, dynamic braking resistors, motor and crane control resistors, generator & battery testing resistors, and harmonic filter resistors.

We design and manufacture our products in compliance with IEEE, ANSI, NEMA, and IEC standards, ensuring maximum safety, performance, and reliability.

Research & Development

Our well-equipped R&D department continuously works on innovation and improvement in electrical resistor technology. We closely follow the latest global developments to deliver advanced and customized solutions.

Our R&D and Quality teams ensure strict testing procedures, product reliability, and complete compliance with customer specifications.

Customization & Client Satisfaction

As manufacturers of Neutral Grounding Resistors and electrical components, we provide fully customized solutions in various dimensions and ratings. Our engineering team works closely with clients to understand their requirements and deliver optimal products.

Client satisfaction is our core objective. Ethical business practices, transparency in production, and consistent product quality have helped us build long-term relationships with our customers.
