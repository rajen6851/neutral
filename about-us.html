About Company

Voltcontrol Engineering Pvt. Ltd., founded in 2023 with 12+ years of expertise in the Power Resistor field, delivers proven solutions for critical industrial and utility applications.

We specialize in neutral grounding resistors and custom-engineered resistor solutions tailored to meet your exact operational requirements.

  • High & Low Resistance Grounding Solutions
  • Neutral Isolation Switch Panels
  • Dynamic Braking & Motor Control Resistors
  • Generator & Battery Testing Resistors
  • Harmonic Filter & Noise Dampening Resistors

Our engineering-driven approach ensures compliance with IEEE, ANSI, NEMA & IEC standards, delivering unmatched safety, reliability, and performance.
