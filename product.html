Our Electrical Products

High-quality electrical solutions made in India for industrial, commercial, and infrastructure applications

Premium Electrical Components

We manufacture and supply a comprehensive range of electrical products designed for reliability, safety, and optimal performance in demanding environments.

Safety Product

Neutral Grounding Resistors (NGR)

Inserted between neutral and ground to provide fault protection. Limits ground fault currents to safe levels. Also called Neutral Earthing Resistors.

  • Limits ground fault current
  • Enhances system safety
  • Prevents equipment damage
Control Panel

Neutral Isolation Switch Panel (NIS)

Safely disconnects neutral for maintenance or testing. Customizable, high-quality, and reliable for industrial use.

  • Safe maintenance operations
  • Customizable configurations
  • High reliability
Testing Equipment

Resistive Load Bank

Simulates electrical loads for testing generators, UPS, and other power equipment. Ensures stable and reliable system operation.

  • Generator testing
  • UPS system validation
  • Power quality assurance
Measurement System

LAVT / LASCPT Panel

Provides precise control and measurement in electrical systems. Ideal for industrial testing and safe operations.

  • Precise measurement
  • Industrial grade
  • Safe operation
Motor Protection

Dynamic Braking Resistor

Dissipates energy during motor braking, improving safety and protecting motors from overvoltage.

  • Motor protection
  • Energy dissipation
  • Overvoltage prevention
Motor Control

Motor Control Resistor

Used for starting, stopping, and controlling motor speeds. Ensures smooth operation and protects motors from electrical stress.

  • Speed control
  • Smooth operation
  • Electrical stress protection
