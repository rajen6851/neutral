Neutral Grounding
(Earthing) Resistors

Neutral Grounding Resistors (NGRs) are used in AC distribution networks to limit earth fault current flowing through the neutral point of transformers and generators.

NERs protect switchgear, generators and transformers by restricting fault current and reducing touch & step potentials, ensuring reliable relay operation with CTs.

Enclosure Materials

The standard enclosure material is Sheet steel minimum 2 mm thickness with an epoxy powder coat finish. Alternative materials include both SS-304 & SS-316 stainless steel. As standard we offer IP55 enclosures. They are suitable for outdoor use whilst also allowing good ventilation. We also supply IP33 and IP54 enclosures. When side mounted cable termination chambers are specified they are IP54 or IP55 as standard. Whilst our standard range of enclosures are suitable for most applications, we are also happy to provide custom designed equipment if your application requires it.
